Our app is designed to help you keep track of your income and expenses, so you always know where your money is going. Wheather you're saving for a goal, managing daily expenses, or just trying to stay within budget, this app gives you the clarity you need

What you can do with the CashFlow app

Overview Dashboard:

View your total balance, income, and expenses in one place.

Transaction Management:

Add new transaction with descriptions, amounts, and categories (Income or Expense).

Validation:

Every transaction is checked to ensure accuracy and consistency.

Data Storage:

Your financial history is saved so you never lose track of your transactions.
